TORTILLAS DE HARINA

Serving Size: 12-15 Cook time: 45min

  • - 2 cups AP Flour
  • - 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • - Salt
  • - 1 stick Butter diced
  • - Enough Hot Water
  • - optional: broth instead of water.

Directions:

mix dry ingridents, mix in butter. Diced means easier to incorporate into flour. add hot water and mix till dough takes shape. should be fast. . if its too sticky add flour, add water if too dry. cover with a towel and let sit for 10 minutes. flour the area you will be rolling. pinch off dough ball, shape a small tortilla then use roller to stretch. tortillas go on to a hot comal, then into a tortilla warmer.

dough can be stored for 3 days, dough can be frozen. tortillas can be stored for a few days before they are too hard to hold and break.
